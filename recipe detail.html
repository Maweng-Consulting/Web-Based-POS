Delicious Chocolate Cake

This chocolate cake is rich, moist, and decadent, perfect for any special occasion. It's easy to make and sure to impress your guests!

Rating: 4.5/5
Share this recipe

Ingredients

  • 1 3/4 cups flour
  • 1 1/2 cups sugar
  • 3/4 cup cocoa powder
  • 1 1/2 tsp baking powder
  • 1 1/2 tsp baking soda
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 cup milk
  • 1/2 cup vegetable oil
  • 2 tsp vanilla extract
  • 1 cup boiling water

Steps

  1. Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C). Grease and flour two 9-inch round baking pans.
  2. In a large bowl, stir together the flour, sugar, cocoa, baking powder, baking soda, and salt.
  3. Add the eggs, milk, oil, and vanilla. Mix for 2 minutes on medium speed.
  4. Stir in the boiling water (batter will be thin). Pour batter into prepared pans.
  5. Bake for 30 to 35 minutes in the preheated oven, until a toothpick inserted into the center comes out clean.
  6. Cool in the pans for 10 minutes, then remove to a wire rack to cool completely.
  7. Frost and enjoy your cake!
